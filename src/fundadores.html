Hayao Miyazaki

He is a Japanese animation film director, producer, animator, writer, illustrator, manga artist, and anime producer. He is also the father of director Gorō Miyazaki. Among his most popular animated films are titles such as Spirited Away, Princess Mononoke and My Neighbor Totoro.

Toshio Suzuki

He is an anime film producer. He was president of Ghibli, a position he held until mid-2008. Currently, for Ghibli he serves as general manager and together with Kiyofumi Nakajima and Koji Hoshino he is part of the representative directors of Ghibli.

Isao Takanata

He was a Japanese director, writer, producer and screenwriter. The Tale of the Princess Kaguya (2013), was his last film, and it was nominated in the category of best animated film at the Oscars. He was diagnosed with lung cancer and died on April 5, 2018 in a Tokyo hospital, at the age of 82.

Yasuyoshi Tokuma

He was a Japanese director and producer, owner of the firm Tokuma Shoten. Spirited Away is one of its main animations. He died on September 20, 2000.
